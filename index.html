About Me

I was born in Ukraine and moved to Portugal at the age of six. When I was around eleven, I got my first computer — and that’s where it all began. I started designing HTML/CSS layouts for platforms like Hi5 and Tumblr, unknowingly taking my first steps into the world of technology. At the time, I wasn’t sure how to further develop this interest, so I stepped away from it for a while. However, the curiosity never really faded.

During my college application process, I wasn’t entirely sure which path to pursue, so I decided to go with a Bachelor's in Accounting and Auditing. While I enjoyed it, I still wasn’t sure if it was the right course for me, so I decided to pursue another Bachelor's, this time in Business Management. For my second cycle of studies, I chose a Master’s in Data Analysis and Decision Supporting Systems, as it combines both technology and business. After building a strong foundation in business, I decided to deepen my programming skills, so I’m currently pursuing a Bachelor's in Informatics Engineering.

On this webpage, you can explore some of my projects, interests, and hobbies.
